Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return Policy
At ABD, our return policy allows for hassle-free returns. You can return eligible items within 30 days of purchase, provided they are unused and in their original condition. We offer refunds to the original payment method, and while there are no restocking fees, please note that customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
